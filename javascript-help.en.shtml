JavaScript activation

JavaScript provides a better navigation experience within EPFL websites. You'll find instructions on how to activate this technology in your browser below.

Internet Explorer

  1. From the Tools menu, click "Internet Options".
  2. Click on the "Security" tab.
  3. Push the "Custom Level..." button.
  4. Scroll to "Scripting" section.
  5. Under "Active Scripting", select the "Enable" radio button and push the OK button.

Firefox

  1. From the Tools menu, click "Options".
  2. Click on the "Content" tab.
  3. Check "Enable Javascript" and push the OK button.

Firefox (MacOSX)

  1. From the "Firefox" menu, click "Preferences".
  2. Click on the "Content" tab.
  3. Check "Enable Javascript" and push the OK button.

Safari (MacOSX)

  1. From the "Safari" menu, click "Preferences".
  2. Click on the "Security" tab.
  3. Check "Enable Javascript".

Safari (Windows)

  1. From the "Modify" menu, click "Preferences"
  2. Click on the "Security" tab.
  3. Check "Enable Javascript".

Opera

  1. From the "Tools" menu, click "Preferences.
  2. Click on the "Advanced" tab.
  3. Click on "Content" in the left pane.
  4. Check "Enable Javascript" and push the OK button.
